Hello there

I'm Alex Monroe,
a visual storyteller.

Light is my language, and every frame I take is a conversation between what is seen and what is felt. I spent two years shooting commercial campaigns before picking up my first wedding assignment — and never looked back.

Whether it's the quiet intimacy of a portrait or the grandeur of a mountain at golden hour, I believe great photography is less about the camera and more about patience, presence, and empathy.
