Hello there

I'm Tash P,
a food photographer.

Food has always been my first love — long before I ever held a camera. I started shooting in restaurant kitchens, learning how chefs think about flavour, texture, and presentation. That same language now lives in every frame I take.

Whether it's the steam rising from a bowl of ramen or the gloss on a perfectly glazed tart, I believe great food photography is about evoking appetite and atmosphere in equal measure.
