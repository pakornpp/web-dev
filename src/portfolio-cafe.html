How it began

A passion for
honest food.

TERROIR began as a small breakfast cart at Chiang Mai's Saturday market. Five years later, it is a beloved neighbourhood institution — still run by the same family, still sourcing from the same farms.

We believe hospitality is not a service. It is a gesture of welcome — from our hands to your table.
